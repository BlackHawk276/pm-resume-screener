Product Manager - Enterprise SaaS

We are seeking an experienced Product Manager to lead the development of our enterprise SaaS platform. This role will be instrumental in defining product strategy, working with cross-functional teams, and delivering exceptional value to our B2B customers.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field
- MBA from a top-tier business school preferred
- 5+ years of product management experience in B2B SaaS environments
- Strong technical background with ability to work closely with engineering teams

Required Experience:
- Minimum 5 years in product management roles
- At least 3 years working on enterprise software products
- Experience with agile development methodologies
- Track record of successful product launches

Key Responsibilities:
- Define and communicate product vision and strategy aligned with company goals
- Develop detailed product roadmaps and prioritize features based on business value
- Work closely with engineering, design, and QA teams throughout the development lifecycle
- Conduct market research and competitive analysis to inform product decisions
- Gather and synthesize feedback from customers, sales, and support teams
- Define success metrics and analyze product performance data
- Present product updates to executive leadership and stakeholders
- Manage product lifecycle from conception through launch and iteration

Must-Have Skills:
- Product strategy and roadmap development
- Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Data analysis and metrics-driven decision making
- SQL and basic analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, Google Analytics)
- Wireframing and prototyping tools (Figma, Sketch)
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Stakeholder management
- Technical aptitude and understanding of software architecture
- User research and customer discovery
- Prioritization frameworks (RICE, value vs. effort)

Nice-to-Have Skills:
- Experience with API products or platform development
- Knowledge of cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Background in design thinking methodologies
- Experience with pricing and monetization strategies
- Familiarity with developer tools and workflows
- Prior experience as a software engineer or technical role
- Knowledge of machine learning or AI applications
- Experience scaling products from 0 to 1 or 1 to 100

Key Competencies:
- Strategic thinking and vision
- Customer-centric mindset
- Data-driven decision making
- Leadership and influence without authority
- Problem-solving and analytical skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Adaptability and comfort with ambiguity
- Ownership and accountability
- Technical depth combined with business acumen
